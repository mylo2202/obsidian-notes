# Case study: Smart Healthcare Institute (SHI)

**Smart Healthcare Institute (SHI)** aims to revolutionise the healthcare services by integrating cutting-edge AI-centric technologies to enhance patient experience, streamline operations, and increase personalised healthcare growth.  
  
**Goal: SHI** aims to stay ahead in the healthcare service market by proposing a state-of-the-art **smart healthcare system** to meet the increasing demand of personalised healthcare in Vietnam.  
  
**Objectives:** It has been suggested to them that the following major features would help them achieve the goal:

- **AI-Powered Recommendation System**: Personalised recommendations based on patient profile and health condition.
- **Smart Appointment and Service Booking**: AI-driven booking of health appointment and services as well as forecasting of the demand (including the numbers of patients and medical staff and resources needed to accommodate them).
- **Automated Customer Support**: Chatbots and virtual assistants to provide 24/7 consultation service.
- **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: AI-powered solutions to dynamically determine service prices in real-time based on demand and market trends.
- **Advanced Fraud Detection**: Machine learning models to detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

Due to the complexity of these features and the fast-pace operations of the business, SHI would like to hire your team as consultant to help them select the suitable features and develop a solution for them.   
  
**Note:** 

- The list of features is **not exhaustive**. You can also suggest to them another major feature (not on the list) that you know would also help them achieve their goal.
- You are **not required to implement the code** for your selected feature. However, **bonus mark** will be given to a successful prototype implementation. 
- Apply the agile approach **up to the detailed design and testing activities**, which include all the design models and specifications. 
- Read the **Group assignment's rubrics** for more detailed marking criteria.
