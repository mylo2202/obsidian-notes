# Group project

Your final assessment is the group project (read the Case study) that you were asked to do from the beginning of the course.

- The project is divided into a number of activities, each of which is set up as a sub-assignment for you to keep track of the progress.
- Read the attached rubric to find out what components you need to submit and their marking rubrics
- At the end, each student team submits 2 deliverables:  
    (1) Jira project link and  
    (2) Confluence workspace link (connected to your Jira project).  
    - you need to add the lecturer (**[le.m.duc@gmail.com](https://mail.google.com/mail/?view=cm&fs=1&to=le.m.duc%40gmail.com&authuser=0)**) as **administrative member** of **both your Jira project and the associated Confluence workspace**

## Rubric

### Project plan (25 pts)

1. Client, problem statement, objectives, details, scope, Jira project plan (agile/scrum), milestones;
2. Jira project plan consists of at least 3 sprints with evidence of the backlog being actively updated and revised

- **Excellent** (25 pts): All elements completed with excellent details and quality
- **Very good** (20 pts): Missing up to 10% of the elements or details
- **Good** (17 pts): Missing up to 30% of the elements or details
- **Pass** (13 pts): Missing up to 50% of the elements or details
- **Unsatisfactory** (7 pts): Missing most of the elements or details

1. **Source code**: Github repo (add my email le.m.duc@gmail.com)
