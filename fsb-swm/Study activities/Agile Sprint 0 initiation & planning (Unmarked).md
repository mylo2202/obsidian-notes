# Agile Sprint 0: initiation & planning (Unmarked)

In your **group**:

- Discuss the case study objectives and choose one objective for your group. An objective leads to the development of a sub-system.
- Find out the requirements of the chosen sub-system. In particular,   
        - find out the specific business values that the system would bring to the client.  
        - determine if Agile is suitable for the development of the subsystem.  
    Besides Google, you can use Google Scholar to search for the relevant academic papers. 
- Refine and update the Jira project with your findings of this task. Write a **project plan** to convince the client of your team's proposal.
- It is expected that your team will update this plan as the project progresses
